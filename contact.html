Kenneth Lorenz is a Toronto-based freelance videographer and photographer.


His interest towards photography came from being a sneakerhead and taking photos of Jordan Shoes during the early years of his migration in Toronto. He was heavily influenced by Massimo Taylor (@_massimoiannone) who was a street photographer in Germany.

His passion towards videography and film began from creating a youtube series and documenting his friend, Kian Terrado, in Vancouver BC for his youtube video "The Redemption".
